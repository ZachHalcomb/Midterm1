Volunteer

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with Animal Friends Humane Society!

Volunteers must attend an orientation prior to assisting us. Call our volunteer connection at 513-867-5727 to hear a recording of upcoming orientations or review the list of upcoming dates below. All orientations are held at the shelter. Our address is 1820 Princeton Road, Hamilton, OH 45011.

Volunteers MUST BE at least 16 years of age. We DO NOT allow anyone under the age of 16 to volunteer regardless of a parent being present.

We’re using SignUp.com to organize our upcoming orientations.
Here’s how it works in 3 easy steps:

  1. Click this link to see our SignUp on SignUp.com: https://signup.com/go/SaUjKed
  1. Review the options listed and choose the spot you would like.
  1. Sign up! It’s Easy – you will NOT need to register an account or keep a password on SignUp.com.

*Note: SignUp.com does not share your email address with anyone.

Upcoming 2021 Volunteer Orientations:

Friday, May 7th from 9am – 10:30am
Saturday, May 8th from 9am-10:30am
Saturday, May 29th from 9am-10:30am

Once oriented, Volunteers can help on their own time with NO SCHEDULE. You can come anytime during our volunteer hours…
M,T,F,Sa,Su 9 AM – 4 PM and W,Th from 11 AM – 6:30 PM.

The more you help the better, and all we ask is that you commit to a minimum of four hours per month. For our Volunteer Program please print and bring our Volunteer Application with you.

*If you require a letterhead to show proof of hours for school, you must submit a request at least 72 hours in advance to AFHS at animalfriendshs@butlercountyohio.org

*PLEASE NOTE If you are required to do court ordered community service you need to submit a Community Service Application in person or via email. If the application is approved, we will contact you to set up your community service orientation. The volunteer program does not cover court ordered community service.
